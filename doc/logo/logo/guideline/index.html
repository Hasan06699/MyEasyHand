Brand Overview

MyEasyHand is a modern, premium home services booking platform built on trust, technology, and simplicity.

Modern

Clean lines, flat design, and mobile-first thinking define our visual language.

Premium

Refined typography and balanced proportions convey quality and professionalism.

Trustworthy

Deep blue anchors the brand with stability; teal signals action and reliability.

Technology Driven

Digital-first design optimized for apps, dashboards, and responsive web.

Safe Area

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least half the icon height on all sides.

No other graphic elements, text, or borders should intrude into the safe area. This ensures the logo remains legible and visually distinct in all contexts.

Minimum Size

To preserve readability, never scale the logo below these minimum dimensions.
